Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures

Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures (also called Barbie: Dreamhouse Adventures or Dream House Adventures and Barbie Dreamhouse Adventures: Go Team Roberts in its 4th and 5th season) is an animated television series by Mattel, the owner of Barbie, to compliment the Barbie: Dreamtopia series.
